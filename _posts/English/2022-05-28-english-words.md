### 词语比较或区别

> well vs fine

well: 身体好
fine：状况良好

```
I'm very well.
I'm fine.
That's fine.
Everything is fine.
```

> woman vs lady

an old woman: 一个老女人，不太友好
an old lady: 一个老太太

> thin vs slim

thin：瘦的
slim：苗条的

夸人用slim
```You are so slim.```
